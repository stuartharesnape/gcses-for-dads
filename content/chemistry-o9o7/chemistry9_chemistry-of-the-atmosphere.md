# Chemistry 9: Chemistry of the Atmosphere

## 1. The Big Idea (30 seconds)

- The atmosphere is a mixture of gases  
- It has changed over time  
- Human activity is now affecting it  
- Some gases trap heat and cause global warming  

---

## 2. Composition of the Atmosphere

Today’s atmosphere contains:

- About 78% nitrogen  
- About 21% oxygen  
- Small amounts of carbon dioxide, water vapour, and noble gases  

Key idea:

- The composition is fairly stable  

---

## 3. How the Atmosphere Has Changed

Early atmosphere (billions of years ago):

- Mainly carbon dioxide  
- Very little oxygen  

Changes over time:

- Volcanoes released gases  
- Oceans formed  
- Carbon dioxide decreased  

Plants and algae:

- Used carbon dioxide for photosynthesis  
- Released oxygen  

Key idea:

- Oxygen levels increased over time  

---

## 4. The Carbon Cycle

Carbon moves between the atmosphere and living things.

Processes include:

- Photosynthesis (removes CO₂)  
- Respiration (releases CO₂)  
- Combustion (releases CO₂)  
- Decay (releases CO₂)  

Key idea:

- Keeps carbon levels balanced  

---

## 5. Greenhouse Gases

These gases trap heat in the atmosphere.

Examples:

- Carbon dioxide (CO₂)  
- Methane (CH₄)  
- Water vapour  

Key idea:

- This is called the greenhouse effect  

---

## 6. Global Warming

Global warming is caused by increased greenhouse gases.

Main cause:

- Burning fossil fuels  

Effects:

- Rising temperatures  
- Melting ice caps  
- Rising sea levels  

---

## 7. Atmospheric Pollutants

Common pollutants:

Carbon monoxide:

- Produced by incomplete combustion  
- Toxic  

Sulfur dioxide:

- Produced by burning fuels with sulfur  
- Causes acid rain  

Particulates:

- Tiny particles in air  
- Cause health problems  

Nitrogen oxides:

- Produced in car engines  
- Contribute to acid rain and smog  

---

## 8. Reducing Pollution

Methods include:

- Using renewable energy  
- Reducing emissions from vehicles  
- Using filters and catalytic converters  

Key idea:

- Aim is to reduce harmful gases  

---

## 9. Check Your Understanding

- What percentage of air is nitrogen? ( 78% )  
- What process removes carbon dioxide? ( photosynthesis )  
- What do greenhouse gases do? ( trap heat )  
- What causes global warming? ( increased greenhouse gases )  
- Name one pollutant gas. ( e.g. carbon monoxide )
