# GCSE Biology (Combined)  
## Chapter 6: Inheritance, Variation and Evolution

---

## 1. The Big Idea (30 seconds)

- Characteristics are passed from parents to offspring  
- Variation exists within species  
- Evolution happens through natural selection  

---

## 2. DNA and Genes

- DNA is the genetic material in cells  
- Found in the nucleus  

Key idea:

- A **gene** is a section of DNA that codes for a characteristic  

---

## 3. Chromosomes

- Long strands of DNA  

Humans have:

- 23 pairs of chromosomes  

Key idea:

- One set from each parent  

---

## 4. Alleles

- Different versions of the same gene  

Example:

- Eye colour gene may have different alleles  

Key idea:

- Alleles cause variation  

---

## 5. Dominant and Recessive Alleles

- **Dominant allele**  
  - Always expressed if present  

- **Recessive allele**  
  - Only expressed if two copies are present  

Example:

- Brown eyes (dominant)  
- Blue eyes (recessive)  

---

## 6. Genetic Diagrams

- Used to predict inheritance  

Example:

- Shows possible combinations of alleles  

Key idea:

- Helps calculate probabilities  

---

## 7. Variation

- Differences between individuals  

Types:

- **Genetic variation**  
  - Caused by genes  

- **Environmental variation**  
  - Caused by surroundings  

Example:

- Height (genes + diet)  

---

## 8. Evolution

- Change in species over time  

Key idea:

- Occurs through natural selection  

---

## 9. Natural Selection

Process:

- Variation exists  
- Some individuals better adapted  
- They survive and reproduce  
- Pass on advantageous genes  

Example:

- Antibiotic resistance in bacteria  

---

## 10. Selective Breeding

- Humans choose organisms with desired traits  

Used for:

- Crops  
- Animals  

Key idea:

- Can lead to reduced genetic variation  

---

## 11. Genetic Engineering

- Changing an organism’s DNA  

Example:

- Inserting genes for desired traits  

Uses:

- Medicine (e.g. insulin production)  
- Agriculture  

---

## 12. Evidence for Evolution

- Fossils  
- Resistant bacteria  
- Observations over time  

Key idea:

- Evolution is supported by scientific evidence  

---

## Check Your Understanding

- What is a gene? (Section of DNA coding for a characteristic)  
- How many pairs of chromosomes do humans have? (23)  
- What is a dominant allele? (Always expressed if present)  
- What is variation? (Differences between individuals)  
- What drives evolution? (Natural selection)
