# GCSE Biology (Combined)  
## Chapter 4: Bioenergetics

---

## 1. The Big Idea (30 seconds)

- Living organisms need energy to survive  
- Plants transfer energy using photosynthesis  
- Cells release energy using respiration  

---

## 2. Photosynthesis

Photosynthesis is how plants make food.

Word equation:

- Carbon dioxide + Water → Glucose + Oxygen  

Key idea:

- Requires light energy and chlorophyll  

---

## 3. How Photosynthesis Works

- Takes place in **chloroplasts**  
- Chlorophyll absorbs light energy  
- Energy is used to convert carbon dioxide and water into glucose  

What happens to glucose:

- Used in respiration  
- Converted into starch for storage  
- Used to make cellulose (cell walls)  
- Used to make amino acids (growth)  

---

## 4. Factors Affecting Photosynthesis

- **Light intensity**  
  - More light → faster rate (up to a limit)  

- **Carbon dioxide concentration**  
  - More CO₂ → faster rate  

- **Temperature**  
  - Higher temperature → faster reactions (up to optimum)  

Key idea:

- The limiting factor controls the rate  

---

## 5. Limiting Factors

- The factor in shortest supply limits the rate  

Example:

- Plenty of light but low CO₂ → CO₂ is the limiting factor  

---

## 6. Respiration

Respiration releases energy from glucose.

Word equation:

- Glucose + Oxygen → Carbon dioxide + Water  

Key idea:

- Happens in all living cells  

---

## 7. Aerobic Respiration

- Requires oxygen  
- Releases a large amount of energy  

Used for:

- Movement  
- Keeping warm  
- Building molecules  

---

## 8. Anaerobic Respiration

Occurs when there is not enough oxygen.

In animals:

- Glucose → Lactic acid  

Effects:

- Causes muscle fatigue  
- Leads to oxygen debt  

In plants and yeast:

- Glucose → Ethanol + Carbon dioxide  

---

## 9. Exercise and Oxygen Debt

During intense exercise:

- Not enough oxygen for aerobic respiration  

So:

- Anaerobic respiration occurs  

After exercise:

- Extra oxygen is needed to remove lactic acid  

Key idea:

- This is called oxygen debt  

---

## 10. Metabolism

- All the chemical reactions in a cell or body  

Includes:

- Respiration  
- Photosynthesis  
- Making new molecules  

Key idea:

- Metabolism keeps organisms alive  

---

- What is respiration? (Releasing energy from glucose)

- What is metabolism? (All chemical reactions in the body)
